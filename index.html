Movie Selector Game

Welcome to the movie selector game!

The game that helps decide who will pick the movie for movie night. Once you hit the start button you will enter your names and pick a genre you would like to watch. Then you will choose a plot from the list that sounds good to you. Both players will then draw a card to decide which movie you will watch on movie night! All face cards are 10 and Aces are high, in the event of a tie, draw again. Have fun and don't forget the popcorn.
